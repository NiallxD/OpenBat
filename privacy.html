Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

Welcome to OpenBat. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the OpenBat software and visit our GitHub repository.

1. Information Collection

OpenBat is an open-source tool. By default, the software itself does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data or usage telemetry on external servers. Everything runs locally on your machine.

2. GitHub Infrastructure

When you visit the OpenBat repository on GitHub, download releases, or interact with issues and pull requests, your interaction is governed by GitHub's own privacy policy. We may see public information you choose to share via GitHub (such as your username when you create an issue).

3. Third-Party Services

If OpenBat uses any third-party APIs (as configured by the user), the privacy policies of those respective services will apply to the data you send them. We recommend reviewing the privacy terms of any third-party services you integrate with this project.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our project or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to open an issue on our GitHub repository.
